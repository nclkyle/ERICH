FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. What are your store hours?

Our store is open during the following hours:
Monday to Saturday: 8 AM - 6 PM
Sunday: 8 AM - 4 PM

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via cash and Gcash.

3. Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery through Lalamove.

4. How much does delivery cost?

The cost of delivery will be based on Lalamove's rates at the time of your order and may change depending on the distance. The customer is responsible for this delivery fee.

5. How can I receive my order?

You can either pick up your order from our physical store or choose delivery to your location.

6. Can I place a wholesale order?

Yes, we cater to both retail and wholesale customers. For wholesale orders, please contact us directly for pricing and bulk order options.

7. How can I ensure the products are fresh?

We ensure that all our meats and seafood are of the highest quality. Products are delivered either fresh or properly frozen to maintain their freshness.
